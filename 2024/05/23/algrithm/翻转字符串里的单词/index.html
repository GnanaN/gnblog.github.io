https://leetcode.cn/problems/reverse-words-in-a-string/description/

思路

1、首先需要去除多余的空格
'the sky is blue'
2、将整个字符串进行翻转
eg：'the sky is blue' --> 'eulb si yks eht'
3、然后找到每一个单词，对该单词进行翻转
eg：'eulb si yks eht' --> 'blue is the sky'
